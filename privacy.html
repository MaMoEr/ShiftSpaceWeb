AR View — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 18 May 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how ShiftSpace ("we", "us") handles information in connection with the AR View mobile application (the "App"). ShiftSpace is the data controller for any personal data processed through the App. You can contact us at privacy@shiftspace.se.

AR View is an augmented-reality viewing application. This policy explains what limited information the App collects, why we collect it, who we share it with, and the rights you have under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. What data we collect

AR View does not require an account. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or any other directly identifying information. The App does not collect or transmit the content you view in augmented reality (such as camera frames, scenes, or models you load).

The App uses Google Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous diagnostic data so we can detect and fix crashes. This is limited to:

  • Device model and operating-system version
  • App version and build number
  • Crash stack traces and non-fatal error reports
  • An anonymised installation identifier generated by Firebase
  • Coarse usage signals such as session start/end and basic event counts

We do not link this diagnostic data to any other information that would let us identify you as an individual.

3. Purpose and lawful basis

We process the data described above to diagnose crashes, fix bugs, understand which app versions are in use, and improve the stability and quality of AR View. The lawful basis for this processing is our legitimate interest under Article 6(1)(f) GDPR in maintaining and improving the App.

We do not use the data for marketing, advertising, profiling, automated decision-making, or any purpose unrelated to operating the App.

4. Data sharing and sub-processors

We share diagnostic data with Google LLC as our sub-processor for crash and analytics infrastructure (Firebase Crashlytics). Google processes this data on our behalf under its Firebase data-processing terms, available at firebase.google.com/terms/data-processing-terms.

We do not sell personal data and we do not share it with any other third parties except where required by law.

5. International transfers

Google may process data outside the European Economic Area (EEA), including in the United States. Such transfers are covered by the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and additional safeguards as described in Google's data-processing terms.

6. Data retention

Crash reports and associated diagnostic data are retained in Firebase Crashlytics for up to 90 days, after which they are deleted in accordance with Google's default retention policy. Aggregated, non-personal statistics may be retained for longer for product analytics.

7. Your rights under GDPR

If you are located in the EU or EEA, you have the following rights regarding personal data we process about you:

  • The right of access (Art. 15)
  • The right to rectification (Art. 16)
  • The right to erasure (Art. 17)
  • The right to restriction of processing (Art. 18)
  • The right to data portability (Art. 20)
  • The right to object to processing based on legitimate interests (Art. 21)

Because AR View does not collect directly identifying information, we may not be able to locate data relating specifically to you without additional details (for example, your anonymised installation identifier). To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@shiftspace.se.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. In Sweden, this is Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten (IMY) — imy.se.

8. Children

AR View is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in jurisdictions where that is the applicable age of digital consent). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective date" above. If we make material changes, we will announce them within the App before they take effect.

10. Contact

Questions or requests about this policy or your personal data can be sent to privacy@shiftspace.se.
